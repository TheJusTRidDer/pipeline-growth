## Email Sending Instructions

1. **Replace `[Your Name]`** with your actual name
2. **Add your email signature** at the bottom
3. **Send one email per day** from each domain (don't blast all 10 at once)
4. **Use a sending tool** (Instantly, Smartlead) or send manually from a warmed-up inbox
5. **Track opens and replies** in your CRM
6. **Log each email** in HubSpot under the contact record
7. **Follow up on replies within 24 hours** (use the scripts from `08-sales-call-scripts.md`)
8. **If no reply in 3 days**, send the first follow-up from the cold email sequence (`03-cold-email-sequence.md`)

**Expected results from 10 personalized emails:**
- Open rate: 60-80% (personalized emails outperform templates)
- Reply rate: 10-20% (specific, relevant insights)
- Discovery calls booked: 1-3
- Clients from this batch: 0-1 (but warms the pipeline for follow-ups)
