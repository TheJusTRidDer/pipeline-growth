Getting Started Guide

1. Add Your Leads

Use Add Lead or Import CSV to bring in prospects. Fill in company, contact, stage, value.

→ Add Lead · Import CSV

2. Move Leads Through Pipeline

Use Pipeline view — drag cards between stages. Track days-in-stage automatically. Leads over 7d get flagged as stale.

→ Go to Pipeline

3. Manage Tasks

Use Tasks to create follow-ups with due dates. Overdue and today tasks show on the Dashboard and Tasks page.

→ Go to Tasks

4. Log Activity Per Lead

Click any lead → Edit modal → use Notes field to log calls, emails, meetings. All activity is timestamped.

→ Go to Leads

5. Track Email Sequences

Email Seq shows 7 templates + per-lead send status. Click "Send Next" to advance through the sequence.

→ Go to Email Seq

6. Analytics & Forecast

Analytics shows weighted pipeline forecast, conversion funnel, and stage velocity. Know your numbers.

→ Go to Analytics
