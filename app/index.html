Getting Started Guide

1. Add Your Leads

Use Add Lead for single entries, or Import to bulk upload via CSV.

→ Add Lead · Import CSV

2. Move Leads Through Pipeline

Use the Pipeline view to see all leads by stage. Click any card to edit details. Track days-in-stage automatically.

→ Go to Pipeline

3. Track Email Sequences

The Email Seq page shows all 7 email templates and per-lead send status. Update email steps as you send follow-ups.

→ Go to Email Seq

4. Export & Spreadsheet

Export your data to CSV for Excel/Sheets. The companion spreadsheet has Pipeline Summary, Email Sequences, Weekly Targets, Monthly Revenue.

→ Export

5. Weekly Review

Check Dashboard every Friday. Track: new prospects (50+), emails sent (200+), replies (5+), calls booked (2+).

→ Go to Dashboard

6. Settings & Data Mgmt

Use the Settings page to reset data, reload demo leads, or clear your activity log.

→ Go to Settings
