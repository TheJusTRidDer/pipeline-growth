## Prioritization Criteria

Sort your 50+ lead list by Priority Score (1-10):

| Score | Criteria | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **9-10** | Perfect ICP + clear buying signal (hiring, funding, new office) | Immediate personalized outreach |
| **7-8** | Strong ICP match, no clear trigger yet | Standard sequence |
| **5-6** | Adjacent industry or slightly outside size range | Include in sequence, test response |
| **1-4** | Poor fit | Remove from active list |

- first.last@company.com
- firstl@company.com
- f.last@company.com
- first@company.co.uk (for UK firms)

## Initial Outbound Priority (First 10 Prospects)

Week 1: Send personalized emails to the 10 highest-scoring prospects.

Personalization for each:
- Mention their firm's specific service/niche
- Reference a recent LinkedIn post or company news
- Connect the insight to their specific industry vertical

Track in CRM:
- Date sent
- Open/click/reply rate
- Follow-up scheduled
