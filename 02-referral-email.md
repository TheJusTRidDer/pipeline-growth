# Existing Client Referral Campaign

## Email 1 — Initial Ask

**Subject:** Quick favor

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

I'm reaching out to a few of my best clients with a quick ask.

You've been a great partner and I'm proud of the results we've achieved together. Since my business grows best through referrals, I'm wondering:

**Do you know 2-3 other firm leaders who face similar growth challenges to what you had before we started working together?**

I'm not looking for a formal introduction — just names and companies. I'll handle the outreach respectfully, and if they become a client, I'll send you a $1,000 thank you (or donate it to a charity of your choice).

Even one name would mean a lot.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
